About FootyGuess
⚽
What is FootyGuess?
A free daily football guessing game. One mystery player per day — use the clues to figure out who it is in 8 guesses or fewer.
🏆
Two daily puzzles
Daily mode — top players from PL, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1 & more.
World Cup 2026 — international stars from all 48 qualified nations.
👥
Player pool
Only well-known players. No obscure squad fillers. Legends like Zidane, Henry & Maldini make occasional appearances.
📅
Data accuracy
Player data reflects the 2025/26 season (accurate as of August 2025). Very recent transfers may not be shown. Positions simplified to GK, DEF, MID, or FWD.
🌍
Nationality
Nationality shows the country a player represents internationally — not necessarily where they were born.
